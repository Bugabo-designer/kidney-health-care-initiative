About Us

The Kidney Health Care Initiative (KHCI) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting kidney health awareness, conducting educational campaigns, and supporting communities in adopting preventive measures against kidney-related diseases. Recognizing the growing prevalence of kidney diseases globally and their impact on individuals and families, KHCI aims to be a leading voice in kidney health advocacy and a bridge to life-saving information and support.

Vision

To ensure that every individual has access to knowledge and resources for maintaining healthy kidneys and preventing kidney-related diseases.

Mission

To promote kidney health through education, advocacy, community engagement, and support programs aimed at reducing the burden of kidney diseases.

Our Campaigns

We organize various campaigns focused on kidney health, including:

  • Free kidney health check-ups
  • Community education seminars
  • Awareness walks and events
  • Distributing educational materials
